## Description 

</center>

**Elixirs** is a mod that rebalance **vanilla potions**, and brings some **other ones** for mods to use. Its purpose is to be both a playable mod, and a potion library for other mods. 
Currently, Elixirs brings **6** new buffs and **20** new debuffs !

It does such things as bringing a more legacy-friendly version of the "**Strength**" effect called Wrath, a nerfed version of the "**Resistance**" effect called Endurance, some 
buffs and debuffs inspired by [Terraria](https://store.steampowered.com/app/105600/Terraria/), the [Calamity Mod](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2824688072) 
and [Lycanite's Mobs](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/lycanites-mobs).

Most of **Elixirs**'s effects can be configured !

##  Added buffs

<br>
<br>
</center>

Elixirs adds 6 new buffs to the game !

|Effect|Description|
|---|---|
|Rejuvenation|Increase the entity's regeneration amount by 50% per level|
|Wrath|Increase the entity's melee damage by 60% per level|
|Endurance|Reduce the entity's taken damage by 10% per level|
|Cleansed|Immune the player to most poisons|
|Friction|Prevents the player from sliding on ice|
|Slippery|Makes the player slide on any block when in a boat|

##  Added debuffs

<br>
<br>
</center>

Elixirs adds 20 new debuffs to the game !

|Effect|Description|
|---|---|
|Crumbling|Increase taken damage by 10% per level|
|Hypercharged|Makes all lightning in a certain radius fall on the afflicted player|
|Burned|Increase taken fire damage by 50% per level|
|Depths Crush|Empty the player's air instantly bar when in water|
|Frozen|Reduce the entity's moving and mining speed by 20% per level when in water|
|Aphagia|Prevents the player from eating or drinking|
|Fear|Prevent the player from inflicting melee damage|
|Deafened|Mute the player's game sounds|
|Bleeding|Prevent the entity from regenerating|
|Smited|Make the entity burn if exposed to the sun|
|Decay|Reduce the entity's regeneration speed by 10% per level|
|Paralisys|Prevent the entity from moving|
|Frozen Solid|Prevent the entity from moving. Add a "Frozen" rendering layer.|
|Symbiosis|Prevent two entities with this effect to harm each other directly|
|Leech|Turns a small portion of taken damage into healing for the attacker|
|Attraction|Force the player to look at the afflicted entity|
|Heavy|Prevent the player from jumping or swimming|
|Unstable|Randomly teleport the player in a small radius on every damage tick|
|Rusted|Slow down the entity for every armor piece worn|
|Shaken|Make the player's screen shake|
